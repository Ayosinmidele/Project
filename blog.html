The Humble Beginning of Jiraiya Sensei

Jiraiya Sensei grew up struggling to become a ninja. His skills were mid and he was overlooked by his collegues. However, he remained very motivated and kept on polishing his skills until he mistakenly summoned the frogs which he wasn't in contract with.

Upon appearing in the village of frogs, the great frog sage who I have forgotten his name saw a vision towards him. The sage foresaw that Jiraiya Sensei will play a huge part in either destroying the world or saving the world depending on his lessons to his future students.

Jiraiya Sensei First Set of Students

During the first great ninja war, Jiraiya Sensei came across some war orphans who were struggling to survive. Jiraiya sensei offered to stay back to take care of them and also decided to train them so as to protect themselves. It was then he noticed one of them possesed the rinnegan, the old sage eyes which appear once in hundred years. The names of the first set of students are:

  • Nagato
  • Yahiko
  • Konan

The Death of Jiraiya Sensei

The death of Jiraiya Sensei was one of the most painful death in Naruto. It was bloody and emotional. To make things worse, he was killed by one of his own students called Nagato otherwise known as Pain as at the time he killed Jiraiya. Stay Wicked Kings!!!
